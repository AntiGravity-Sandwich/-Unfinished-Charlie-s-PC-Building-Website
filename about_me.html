About Me

Hello, my name is Charlie! I'm only 15, but I am a big PC enthusiast and have spent the last few years learning about computers, components, and how they work. I like to focus on the hardware side. I love computer building and decided mid 2025 that I wanted to build them professionally. This contributed to me making this website and offering the service of building a custom computer for anyone who would like one.

I'm 15 and born and raised in rural Wisconsin. Due to lack of things to do, I started playing and enjoying videogames and messing around with tech. I started small with circuit toys and robots, which evolved into modding consoles such as my family's Wii, PS2 and later Xbox 360. Later I got into PC building youtubers and built my first PC in August 2025. I loved the process and continue to learn more all of the time. So I am launching my business so I can:

1.Enjoy my hobby on a professional level

2.Make money for college after high school

3.Prove to my parents I can actually make money doing this ;]
