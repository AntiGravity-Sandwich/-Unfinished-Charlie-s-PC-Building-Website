Recommended Websites

These are the websites I recommend for accurate information and parts!

I have used these websites for a few years and they have never let me down!

Recommended Websites

PC Part Picker -This site is great for picking and comparing parts, it also has a great compatibility checker and a price comparison tool. I use this site to build my PC's and to check for compatibility and prices.

Newegg - This site is great for buying parts, they have a wide selection of parts and they often have great deals and you can save a lot with bundles.

Tom's Hardware - This site is a great resource for reviews and benchmarks, they have a wide range of reviews and benchmarks for all kinds of parts and they are very detailed and accurate. I use this site to research parts and to see how they perform in real-world scenarios.

Reddit - PCMasterRace - This subreddit is a great place to ask questions and get reliable and helpful advice from the community and share memes.
